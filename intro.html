this course is under construction.

Course targets#

  • AUDIENCE: ML practicioners with a basic understading of Tensorflow

  • FOCUS:

    • Probability concepts are simple but profound. We will spend time building strong intuitions on probability.

    • We will use Tensorflow Probability (TFP) to build learnable probabilistic models.

    • We will focus on the why (using probabilistic models) as much as on the how (implementing them in TFP and learning them from data).

    • The course will be online with self graded labs to do at your pace.
